About Us

NERO AVENUE is a network to connect with people who are addicted to the colour black. It’s a virtual platform committed to share, curate, and connect with fellow NERO Addicts who bring their own sensorial stories. A nook to provoke inspiration and enjoy the shades of NERO in art, design, fashion, beauty, food and photography. Simply put, NERO Avenue is a global community of professionals and enthusiasts who all see black as more than a mere colour.
